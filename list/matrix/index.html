The Matrix


Description:


Neo (Keanu Reeves) believes that Morpheus (Laurence Fishburne),
an elusive figure considered to be the most dangerous man alive,
can answer his question -- What is the Matrix? Neo is contacted by
Trinity (Carrie-Anne Moss), a beautiful stranger who leads him into
an underworld where he meets Morpheus. They fight a brutal battle
for their lives against a cadre of viciously intelligent secret
agents. It is a truth that could cost Neo something more
precious than his life.
